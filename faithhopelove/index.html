Our goal is to sponsor one child at a time and help the child in any way we can to achieve a better life.

Mission

To provide children in areas of the world where there are little or no opportunities to receive an education a way to achieve their dreams. Our goal is simple. We sponsor one child at a time and help the child in any way we can to achieve a better life.

Currently

We are working and sponsoring street children in Kolkata, India to obtain an education with full room and board so that they may eventually be able to support themselves and their families such that they donâ€™t have to live in the streets sorting trash in landfills or being forced into prostitution.
